MOSAIC Methods Workshop

MOSAIC offers graduate students an additional space to workshop research, acquire new skills, and sharpen understanding of social science research methods—across qualitative, quantitative, and computational traditions.

What is MOSAIC? This Semester

About the Workshop

MOSAIC is a department-based methods community. We run weekly sessions that rotate between: paper workshops, methods demos, research design clinics, reading groups, and professionalization topics. The goal is simple: help you make faster progress on higher-quality work, together.

  • Open to all graduate students in Political Science (and allied fields by arrangement).
  • Bring work-in-progress: drafts, designs, data, or even ideas on a napkin.
  • We keep it constructive, specific, and time-bound.
