About Schedule

About the Workshop

MOSAIC is a department-based methods community. We run weekly sessions that rotate between: research presentations, methods demos, programming modules, and student labs.

  • Open to all faculty and graduate students in the Department of Political Science.
  • Bring work-in-progress: drafts, designs, data.
  • Keep it constructive and specific.
